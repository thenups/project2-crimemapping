Problem Statement

Explore the correlation between violent crime (type and volume) and school shootings from 1990 to 2014.

Methodology

Step 1 - Analyze state data for each year and crime type

Step 2 - Evaluate school shooting jurisdiction data

Step 3 - Find reliable, manipulateable data sources

Step 4 - Determine next steps if no correlation exists

Definitions

Violent crimes - murder, assault, kidnapping, burglary.

SNAP - Supplemental nutrition assistance program (ala food stamp program).

Economic distress indicators - foreclosure rate, SNAP participation rate, employment rate.
